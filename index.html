About

TransMapper is a light weight Census Mapper based on Javascript libraries d3.v2.min.js , cartogram.js , natural.js , topojson.js , jquery-1.10.2.min.js and colorbrewer.js. See details.
TransMapper "transforms" our way of mapping the census by providing a cartogram-based visualization and a simple natural language interface.

Based on a previous example (shamelessly borrowed from Shawn Allen), TransMapper is designed and built by Wei Chen (David), Bo Zhao & Xu Zhe within 25 hours for the OSUHackathon competition.
